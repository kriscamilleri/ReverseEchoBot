Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2025

1. Who we are

This privacy notice applies to the Reverse Echo Bot, a proof‑of‑concept Microsoft Teams bot operated for internal testing and demonstration purposes only.

2. What data we process

  • Message content – the text you send to the bot.
  • Service metadata – IDs, timestamps, and channel information automatically supplied by Microsoft Teams.

3. How we use the data

The bot reverses the incoming text and returns it immediately. No personal data is profiled, shared, or sold.

4. Storage & retention

Message content is held in volatile memory only while the response is generated, then discarded. No persistent logs are kept.

5. Your rights

Because no data is retained, requests for access, correction, or deletion are fulfilled automatically upon message completion.

6. Contact

If you have questions, email dev@example.com.
